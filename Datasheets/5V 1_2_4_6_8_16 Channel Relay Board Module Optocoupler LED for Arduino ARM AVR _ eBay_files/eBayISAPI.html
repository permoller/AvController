Description
 
Relay modules suitable for switching of higher voltages and perfect for use with microcontrollers including Arduino, ARM, AVR and PIC. The relays used are rated up to 10A and have NO/NC contacts. Each channel has an LED indicator to show the relay is on.
Channels: 1,2,4 or 8
Connects directly to a microcontroller
Switching Capacity: 10A
Maximum Voltage: 250VAC / 30VDC
 
Size:
1-Channel with Optocoupler Dimensions:53mm*28.3mm*19.3mm

2-Channel with Optocoupler Dimensions: 51 x 39 x 18mm (L x W x H)

4-Channel with Optocoupler Dimensions: 76 x 56 x 18mm (L x W x H)

8-Channel with Optocoupler Dimensions: 138 x 56 x 18mm (L x W x H)
 
1-Channel without Optocoupler （Low level trigger ）
1-Channel without Optocoupler High （High level trigger）

16CH without Optocoupler




Note:just 3 modules are without Optocoupler.
